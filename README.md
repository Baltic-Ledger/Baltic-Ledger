# Baltic Hedger: Automated Compliance & Risk Mitigation for Green H2

**Baltic Hedger** is a specialized compliance-as-a-service (CaaS) platform designed for the 2026 European energy market. We provide the digital infrastructure to bridge the Indo-Baltic hydrogen corridor, ensuring every kilogram of imported fuel is legally "Green" and financially optimized.

## Core Technology
* **RED III Compliance Engine:** Automated validation against the EU threshold of **28.2 gCO2e/MJ**.
* **Temporal Matching Logic:** Hourly correlation of renewable energy (PPA) vs. electrolyzer load.
* **CBAM Liability Forecast:** Live financial modeling of Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism costs based on **Feb 2026 EU ETS** market pricing.
* **Well-to-Gate Traceability:** Generation of unique, immutable "Sustainability Passports."
